poetry and writing

Welcome to my publications page! For two years in middle school, I was really into writing poetry. Some people turn to art and music to convey their emotions, postive or negative. Despite loving to sketch and play my instruments, I found myself turning to writing, something that I felt I didn't exactly "excel" at. My love for writing grew over the years, and I love doing it, as long as I have inspiration and ideas in mind. Below are some of my favorite poems that I have written back then.

After I somewhat outgrown writing poetry, I picked up something else. It started with a simple writing assignment about mood in my 9th grade English class. Since then, I began to pay more attention to the moods of the songs I listen to and what kind of scenario my head makes up in the meantime. Sometimes, random inspiration hits, and I just feel the need to turn it into a story if the inspiration is great enough. These are just short (really short) stories/excerpts that I have written over the past year or so, just for fun and to practice writing.
